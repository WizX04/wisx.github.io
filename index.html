About Studio Ghibli

Studio Ghibli is a renowned Japanese animation studio founded in 1985 by Hayao Miyazaki, Isao Takahata, and Toshio Suzuki. Known for its breathtaking visuals and heartfelt storytelling, Studio Ghibli has created timeless masterpieces that captivate audiences worldwide.

Hayao Miyazaki

The visionary director behind iconic films such as My Neighbor Totoro and Spirited Away.

Isao Takahata

A masterful director known for his emotional storytelling in films like Grave of the Fireflies.

Toshio Suzuki

The producer who brought Studio Ghibli's vision to life, ensuring its global success.

A Brief History

Studio Ghibli's journey began with its first feature film, Castle in the Sky (1986). Over the years, it has produced globally acclaimed films like Spirited Away, which won the Academy Award for Best Animated Feature in 2003. The studio continues to inspire creativity and celebrate the wonders of life through its magical storytelling.

Studio Ghibli's Top Picked Movies

Explore the magic of Studio Ghibli's most iconic films, each one a masterpiece of animation and storytelling.

Castle in the Sky

An orphan girl and a boy with a mysterious crystal pendant embark on a journey to find a legendary floating castle, pursued by pirates and secret agents.

Howl's Moving Castle

Sophie, a young woman cursed by a witch into an old woman's body, seeks refuge in a wizard's magical moving castle and becomes entangled in a war of sorcery.

Ponyo

A young goldfish princess named Ponyo escapes the ocean and becomes friends with a human boy, but her transformation upsets the balance of nature.

Spirited Away

Chihiro, a ten-year-old girl, stumbles into a magical world where her parents are transformed into pigs, and she must work in a bathhouse for spirits to save them.

The Boy and the Heron

A young boy dealing with the loss of his mother discovers a magical heron and embarks on a surreal journey into a parallel world of mystery and healing.

My Neighbor Totoro

Two sisters move to the countryside to be near their ailing mother and encounter magical creatures, including the lovable forest spirit Totoro.

The Wind Rises

The story of Jiro Horikoshi, the designer of Japanese fighter planes during World War II, and his pursuit of dreams and love in turbulent times.
