Totoro's 35th Anniversary Celebration Announced!

Studio Ghibli has announced a major global event to celebrate the 35th anniversary of the iconic film "My Neighbor Totoro." The celebration will include special screenings of the film in theaters worldwide, showcasing the timeless appeal of this beloved animated masterpiece. In addition to the screenings, Ghibli will host a series of exhibitions featuring original artwork, concept designs, and behind-the-scenes footage, giving fans a unique opportunity to dive deeper into the creative process behind the film. Limited-edition merchandise will also be released, featuring classic Totoro designs and new artwork to commemorate the anniversary.

Published on: April 04, 2023

Spirited Away Stage Adaptation Wins Prestigious Award

The stage adaptation of Studio Ghibli's "Spirited Away" has garnered critical acclaim and won the award for Best Theatrical Production of 2024. Premiering earlier this year, the stage production brought the magical world of the film to life, with stunning visual effects, elaborate sets, and powerful performances that captivated audiences of all ages. The adaptation was praised for its ability to remain faithful to the original animation while making the transition to the stage in an innovative and visually captivating way. The production's success has led to an extended run and has sparked new interest in the classic film, as well as future theatrical projects from Studio Ghibli.

Published on: September 13, 2024

Ghibli Museum Unveils New Exhibit: The Making of Ponyo

The Ghibli Museum in Mitaka, Japan, has unveiled a new exhibit dedicated to the creation of "Ponyo," one of Studio Ghibli’s most beloved films. The exhibit takes visitors behind the scenes of the film’s creation, offering a unique glimpse into the production process of the studio’s masterful animation techniques. Featuring original sketches, concept art, and even animatics, the exhibit explores how the film’s vibrant underwater world was brought to life with incredible attention to detail. Interactive displays allow visitors to learn about the voice acting, sound design, and animation techniques that made "Ponyo" a modern Ghibli classic. Fans can also view exclusive behind-the-scenes footage of Hayao Miyazaki and the team at work, making this exhibit a must-see for any Ghibli fan.

Studio Ghibli's Latest Released Movies

Experience the wonder of Studio Ghibli's legendary films, each a brilliant fusion of animation and storytelling.

When Marnie Was There

A beautifully haunting tale of a young girl named Anna who befriends Marnie, a mysterious girl with a secret past. As Anna uncovers the truth behind their bond, she embarks on a journey of self-discovery and healing.

Earwig and the Witch

In this CGI-animated film, Earwig, a clever and independent young girl, is adopted by a strange couple—an evil witch and a talking cat. With her wits and resourcefulness, Earwig navigates her new life, determined to control her destiny.

The Boy and the Heron

A deeply magical story from Hayao Miyazaki, following a young boy’s journey through grief and wonder after the death of his mother. As he discovers a mystical world full of fantastical creatures, he learns about life, death, and the bonds that tie us all together.
